Rec.bio

A fully responsive website for a startup company whose goal is to deliver modern eco-friendly solutions for your home. I was responsible for both design and execution, as well as preparing all graphic materials, such as the landing photo, vector icons, backgrounds and section separators. In order to minimize the number of requests to the server, the site makes use of svg sprites. It is still being developed and perfected.
